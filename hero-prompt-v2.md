# Intent First — Hero Image Prompt v2: Abstract Direction

## Why Not a Compass

The compass metaphor implies the user still has to choose a direction.
Intent First is more radical than that — the user's intent EXISTS,
and the environment transforms itself in response. No choosing, no navigating.
The machine reads, interprets, and acts.

This is closer to: gravity, magnetism, a field of influence, resonance.

---

## Core Visual Concept: "The Field"

人間のインテントは「選択」ではなく「場」として存在する。
その場に入ったものは、自然と形を変え、最適化される。
重力のように、磁場のように、存在するだけで周囲を変える力。

## Prompt Set A: "Gravity Well" (重力場)

### A-1: The Singularity (特異点)

```
A single luminous point of warm amber light at the center of a vast dark space.
Around it, translucent glass-like geometric fragments — cubes, spheres,
triangular prisms, thin rectangular panels — are being drawn inward,
curving their trajectories toward the amber core.
Some fragments closer to the center have already begun to reshape,
becoming more ordered, more structured, more crystalline.
Fragments far from the center are chaotic and scattered.
Near the center, they align into perfect geometric harmony.
The amber light is soft but absolute — everything orbits around it.
No human figure. The light IS the intent.
Photorealistic 3D render, dark background (#08080a),
iridescent blue glass with subtle amber and purple refractions,
volumetric god rays emanating from center, shallow depth of field,
cinematic ultra-wide 21:9 composition.
```

### A-2: The Convergence (収束)

```
Hundreds of tiny translucent blue glass particles streaming through
dark space, all converging toward a single bright amber sphere at center.
As particles approach the sphere, they transform — from random scattered dust
into organized geometric structures: clean lines, perfect angles, crystalline forms.
The outer edges of the image are chaos. The center is pure order.
The amber sphere pulses with a warm glow, acting as the organizing force.
Trails of iridescent light follow each particle's path.
Extreme depth of field — sharp center, soft bokeh edges.
Dark moody background, octane render quality, 8K detail.
Ultra-wide 21:9 aspect ratio, center-weighted composition.
```

## Prompt Set B: "Phase Transition" (相転移)

### B-1: Crystallization

```
A large abstract form suspended in dark space, caught in the moment of
crystallization. The left side is amorphous — soft, cloudy, undefined
translucent glass with no clear shape. Moving toward the right,
the form gradually solidifies into sharp, precise geometric crystal structures
with clean facets and perfect angles. The crystalline side glows
with warm amber light from within, while the amorphous side is cool blue.
The transition zone between chaos and order shimmers with iridescent refractions.
This single object represents the moment intent becomes structure.
Photorealistic 3D render, dark background, volumetric lighting,
caustics and light refractions through glass material.
Ultra-wide 21:9. Left side has visual space for text overlay.
```

### B-2: Ice to Diamond

```
A flowing stream of translucent blue liquid glass pours from the top left
of the frame, and as it reaches the center, it flash-freezes into
a perfect geometric crystal structure — sharp edges, clean facets,
amber light trapped inside like an ember. Small crystalline fragments
float around the main structure, some still mid-transformation.
The liquid represents raw, unstructured human context.
The crystal represents structured, machine-readable intent.
Dark void background, cinematic lighting from the amber core,
iridescent refractions on all glass surfaces.
Ultra-wide 21:9, main structure positioned right of center.
```

## Prompt Set C: "Resonance" (共鳴)

### C-1: Standing Wave

```
In a vast dark space, a series of translucent glass concentric rings
ripple outward from a bright amber point of light at center —
like a stone dropped in still water, but frozen in 3D.
The rings are made of iridescent blue glass, each one progressively
larger and more complex in geometric detail.
The innermost ring is a simple circle. The outermost rings have
evolved into intricate crystalline lattice structures.
Between the rings, tiny glass fragments hover in suspension,
caught in the resonance field.
The composition suggests that from one single point of intent,
infinite structure radiates outward.
Dark background, volumetric light from center, octane render,
shallow depth of field on the outer rings.
Ultra-wide 21:9, center-weighted.
```

### C-2: Harmonic Field

```
Abstract 3D visualization of an invisible force field made visible.
In dark space, thousands of tiny blue glass spheres are arranged
in a flowing pattern — dense and perfectly ordered near the center
(where a warm amber glow exists), becoming progressively more scattered
and random toward the edges. The pattern resembles magnetic field lines
made physical. Each sphere catches light differently, creating
an ocean of iridescent blue pinpoints against the darkness.
Near the center, the spheres form recognizable geometric patterns.
At the edges, pure entropy.
One single amber sphere at the origin — brighter, warmer than all others.
This is intent. Everything else responds to it.
Photorealistic 3D render, tilt-shift effect, dark background,
volumetric ambient light.
Ultra-wide 21:9 aspect ratio.
```

## Prompt Set D: "The Bloom" (開花)

### D-1: Unfolding

```
A single geometric seed — a small, dense amber crystal — sits at the center
of the frame in dark space. From it, layers of translucent blue glass
petals and geometric panels are unfolding outward in all directions,
like a flower blooming in slow motion. Each layer is more complex
than the last — the innermost are simple triangles, the outer layers
are intricate lattice structures with hexagonal and octagonal patterns.
The unfolding is frozen mid-motion, some panels still rotating into place.
Light refracts through each translucent layer, creating rainbow caustics
on the surfaces behind them.
This represents intent as a seed that, once it exists,
naturally unfolds into full contextual structure.
Dark background, photorealistic 3D, volumetric backlighting.
Ultra-wide 21:9, generous space on left for text overlay.
```

## Production Notes

### What These All Share:
1. **No human figure** — intent is represented as pure light/energy
2. **Chaos → Order narrative** — disorder transforms into structure near the "intent" point
3. **Amber = Intent, Blue = Context/Structure** — consistent with site palette
4. **Dark background** — seamless with the dark hero section
5. **Left-side breathing room** — space for headline text overlay
6. **No compass, no specific symbol** — fully abstract

### Recommended Priority:
1. **B-1 "Crystallization"** — Most visually clear metaphor, easiest to read
2. **A-2 "The Convergence"** — Dynamic, energetic, cinematic
3. **D-1 "Unfolding"** — Elegant, organic, unique
4. **C-1 "Standing Wave"** — Beautiful but may read as too "scientific"
