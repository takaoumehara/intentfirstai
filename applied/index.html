Each project applies the 8 tokens to a real scenario. Not mockups — working prototypes that show what happens when AI understands human context.

01

The Living Home

⑤ Priority Weight ⑥ Synchro Rate ⑧ Feasibility

Scenario

Business traveler, flight cancelled at 11pm, exhausted. It's 28 degrees outside, you're 2 hours from home, and you need to decide: rebook a red-eye, catch a train in the morning, or rent a car. Your brain is fried. You don't want options — you want the answer.

What happens

Lock screen becomes a resolution dashboard. No searching, no app-switching. The system reads your Cognitive Load (panic + exhaustion), your Priority Weight (get home, not comfortable), and your Feasibility (based on real flight prices, train schedules, available cars). It evaluates all three options in context — not just their travel times, but which one is most possible for you right now. It surfaces one. Maybe two. That's it. The interface dissolves everything except the answer.

Key insight

When Cognitive Load = panic, maxOptions = 1. The interface adapts to your mental state. More choices in this moment would paralyze you further. The design contract changes: instead of giving you optionality, it gives you clarity.

02

The Family Trip

① Intent ⑧ Feasibility ⑦ Form Factor

Scenario

You're driving home, 28 minutes until dinner. Your family is hungry. Your fridge is half-stocked — you have chicken, rice, and time. But you're missing a lemon for the sauce. Do you make something else? Stop at a store? Or is there a third way the system can see?

What happens

AI checks the smart fridge inventory in real-time, cross-references 12 recipes against what's available, auto-orders the missing lemon via Instacart (15-minute delivery, $1.29). By the time you walk into the kitchen, the Instacart notification is there, the recipe is already on the kitchen display, and you're ready to start. The Intent token (make a great dinner) gets satisfied. The Feasibility token makes sure it's actually possible given your constraints. Nothing ideal is proposed — only what's real.

Key insight

Feasibility Token turns AI from "ideal recommender" to "real execution partner." A recipe recommendation is nice. A system that rewires the supply chain to make that dinner possible is transformative. The interface doesn't judge — it executes.

03

Fluid Handoff & Shared Arbitration

② Physical ④ Social Exposure ⑦ Form Factor

Scenario

Saturday afternoon shopping with your partner. You're on the couch with them, looking at shoes on the TV (big screen, visuals matter). Your partner has their phone. Then you get a work call — you take the train home alone. The experience needs to transform, not break.

What happens

On the couch, TV displays high-res product images at scale. Phone is a remote control — swipe left/right to browse, tap to zoom. Prices and specs on the TV. Then you board the train. Suddenly your phone knows: Physical State changed (sitting → standing + transit), Social Exposure changed (private home → public crowded train). The UI transforms. Single-hand mode activates. Prices blur (you don't want to broadcast your budget in public). The TV grid collapses into a vertical feed. The phone takes over the entire experience. When you stop moving and enter a quiet space, the interface opens back up. All this happens without you tapping a settings menu.

Key insight

Form Factor + Physical State + Social Exposure together reshape the entire interface for every context change. The system doesn't need a "mobile view" and a "TV view" — it needs to understand which form factors are available, how you're physically positioned, and how exposed you are. Then it draws the UI live.
