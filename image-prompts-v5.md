## 1. Token Intro — "The Gap" (イントロセクション用)

### Image A: "What Your Phone Knows" (スマホが知っていること)
```
Isometric illustration on light gray background. Center: a smartphone on a small isometric platform, surrounded by floating data cards connected by thin blue lines. Each card shows a simple icon: a map pin (location), a calendar grid (schedule), a search magnifying glass (search history), a shopping bag (purchase history), a clock (time). The cards are bright, crisp, well-organized — representing structured data. Style: blue and white palette with orange accents. Black silhouette figure sitting at desk looking at phone. Clean, professional, minimal. No text on the illustration.
```

### Image B: "What Your Phone Doesn't Know" (スマホが知らないこと)
```
Isometric illustration on light gray background. Same smartphone on platform as Image A, but now surrounded by fuzzy, semi-transparent, amorphous cloud-like shapes (not cards). Each cloud vaguely suggests a human state: a tired face silhouette, a running figure, a crowd of people, a brain with scattered dots, shaking hands. The clouds are blurred, unstructured, with dashed outlines — representing invisible human context that devices cannot read. A visible gap (empty space with a subtle dotted bridge) separates the phone from these clouds. Blue and muted purple palette. Same black silhouette figure, now standing on a train holding a strap, looking exhausted. No text.
```

### Image C: "Context Grammar Bridges the Gap" (Context Grammarがギャップを埋める)
```
Isometric illustration on light gray background. Left side: the fuzzy amorphous clouds from Image B (human states). Right side: the crisp data cards from Image A (device data). In the center: a glowing bridge or pipeline made of 8 small geometric tokens (cubes, spheres, prisms) in blue and orange, arranged in a flowing line connecting the two sides. The tokens transform the fuzzy clouds into structured signals that flow into the phone. A black silhouette figure stands confidently on the bridge. The overall feel is: chaos becoming order, human context becoming machine-readable. Blue, orange, and white palette. No text.
```

## 2. Before/After Team Structure (チーム構造のビフォー・アフター)

### Image D: "Before — The Execution Relay" (従来のチーム)
```
Isometric illustration on light gray background. A long assembly-line style workflow on connected isometric platforms. 5 black silhouette figures stand on separate platforms in a chain, each with a distinct prop:

Platform 1 (leftmost): PM figure at a desk with a document/clipboard (writing specs)
Platform 2: Designer figure at a screen showing a grid/wireframe (making mockups)
Platform 3: Content figure at a typewriter or text editor
Platform 4: Developer figure at a computer with code brackets on screen
Platform 5: QA figure with a magnifying glass inspecting a checklist

Blue connecting arrows flow left-to-right between each platform, showing the hand-off relay. The arrows are rigid, straight, mechanical. The overall feel is: linear, sequential, slow, each person doing one narrow task and passing it forward. Blue and light gray palette, orange accent on the arrows. No text.
```

### Image E: "After — Context & Judgment" (AI時代のチーム)
```
Isometric illustration on light gray background. A circular/hub arrangement instead of a line. In the center: a large glowing AI core — represented as a luminous blue geometric polyhedron (like the hero image's crystal) sitting on a central platform. Around it in a circle: 3-4 black silhouette figures on connected platforms, each with a distinct role:

Figure 1: Product Strategist — standing at a holographic dashboard showing the 8 token icons, defining context
Figure 2: AI-Augmented Engineer — sitting at a console with flowing data streams connecting to the AI core
Figure 3: Design/Taste Lead — reviewing multiple AI-generated screens on a floating display, selecting/rejecting with gestures
Figure 4 (optional): Validation Engineer — with a shield/checkmark icon, monitoring outputs

Orange glowing bi-directional arrows connect each figure to the central AI core. The overall feel is: collaborative, circular, everyone connected to AI, larger scope. The AI core is doing the execution; humans are orchestrating. Blue and orange palette. The figures' platforms are slightly elevated compared to the "Before" image, suggesting higher-level work. No text.
```
