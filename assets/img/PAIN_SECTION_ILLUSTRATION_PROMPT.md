# Pain Section Illustration Prompt

## For: Context Grammar page — "Your phone has no idea what you're going through" section

### Art Style (match existing illustrations)
Isometric / 2.5D perspective. Black silhouette human figures (no facial features). Blue (#2563EB) and orange (#D97706) accent colors for UI elements and thought bubbles. Light warm gray (#EDEDEB) background. Clean, minimal, geometric shapes. Thin linework.

### Scene Description
A dad driving a car (seen from slightly above, isometric angle). Two small kid silhouettes visible in the back seat. The phone is mounted on the dashboard — its screen glowing blue.

Around the driver's head, show **4 floating app windows / thought bubbles** in a chaotic arrangement, each representing a different mental task:
1. A recipe app window (orange accent) with a long scrolling list — implying "too many results"
2. A fridge icon with question marks — implying "what do I even have?"
3. A delivery app with a clock/timer — implying "will it arrive in time?"
4. Small icons of a mushroom with an X and a bread with an X — implying "family dietary restrictions"

The overall feeling should be: **one person juggling too many decisions, overwhelmed, while driving.** The phone on the dashboard looks small and unhelpful compared to the mental chaos above.

### Composition
- Car occupies the center-right of the frame
- Thought bubbles / app windows float above and to the left
- Light gray background, no heavy scenery — keep it clean
- Aspect ratio: roughly 4:3 or 16:10 (landscape)

### Mood
Slightly stressful but not dark. The chaos is relatable, not frightening. Think "modern life complexity" — too many apps, not enough intelligence.
