# Intent First — Hero Image: "Chaos to Structure"

## Concept (Takaoのビジョン)

左側: ふわふわとした、まだ整合性のない人間の思考やインテントが宙に浮いている。
ランダムで、曖昧で、形が定まっていない。
右側: AIの力によって、それらが整然とした、しっかりしたオブジェクトとして成立している。
構造化され、明確で、美しい。

この変化を一枚の画像の中で、左→右の流れとして見せる。

---

## Prompt for Gemini Gem (Gem Instruction準拠)

### Main Prompt:

```
A transformation scene showing chaos becoming order, from left to right.

On the left side: soft, cloudy, amorphous shapes floating in the air —
blurred glass-like forms with undefined edges, semi-transparent,
overlapping randomly. Some resemble half-formed thoughts:
a vague speech bubble shape, a blurry calendar-like rectangle,
a melting clock form, scattered dots and wisps.
These are translucent, cool-toned — pale blue, soft lavender,
with frosted glass surfaces. They drift without purpose.

In the center: a subtle transition zone where the amorphous forms
begin to sharpen. Edges start appearing. Colors intensify.
Glass surfaces become clearer, more refractive.

On the right side: the same forms have crystallized into
precise, structured geometric objects — clean-edged cubes,
a perfect octagonal prism, neatly stacked blocks,
a clear spherical form. These are made of thick, sculptural
iridescent glass with strong holographic surfaces.
Colors shift to warm amber, rose gold, and deeper blue.
They are arranged with intentional spacing, like a curated collection.

The entire scene floats above a smooth reflective surface
with soft caustic light patterns below.
The transformation reads clearly from left (chaos) to right (order).
```

### Shorter Alternative (シンプル版):

```
Left to right transformation of glass objects floating above a reflective surface.

Left side: 5-6 soft, cloudy, amorphous translucent glass blobs
with undefined shapes, cool blue and lavender tones, frosted and blurry,
drifting randomly.

Right side: 5-6 precise, sharp geometric glass objects —
cubes, prisms, spheres — made of thick iridescent glass
with holographic amber, rose, and blue surfaces,
arranged in perfect order.

Center: the transition zone where blobs are mid-transformation
into geometric forms. One object is half-amorphous, half-crystallized.

Soft studio lighting, light blue-gray background, caustic reflections below.
```

### Dramatic Version (よりドラマチック):

```
A single continuous glass form that stretches horizontally across the frame.

The left end is organic, melted, chaotic — like a cloud of molten glass
frozen mid-explosion, cool blue and violet iridescence,
no defined edges, beautiful but formless.

The right end is architectural — the same glass has solidified into
a precise geometric structure with flat facets, sharp angles,
and warm amber-gold iridescence. It looks intentional, designed, purposeful.

The transition happens gradually across the middle —
you can see the exact point where chaos becomes structure.
The material is the same throughout — thick sculptural glass —
but its state transforms from disordered to ordered.

One continuous object. Light blue-gray background.
Soft studio lighting. Strong caustic patterns on the reflective surface below.
```

## Why This Works for Intent First

この画像が伝えるストーリー：

「あなたの頭の中にある、まだ言葉にもなっていない曖昧な意図 —
それをContext Grammarが構造化し、AIが理解できる形に変える。」

- 左 = ユーザーの生の思考（言語化されていないインテント）
- 中央 = Context Grammar（翻訳のプロセス）
- 右 = AIが理解できる構造化されたコンテキスト

コンパスも人間もない。純粋に「変容」そのものを見せる。
